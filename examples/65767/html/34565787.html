Of the five species here included in Hol-
otheres, the types of two species, H. sem-
peri (Burger, 1895) and H. flavus (Nauck,
1880), are considered to be lost. The tax-
onomy of only H. semperi and H. flavus are
discussed herein. Holotheres setnai has
been well described and figured by Chopra
(1931). Holotheres halingi has been de-
scribed and figured in detail by Hamel et
al. (1999). Holotheres villosissimus (see
Doflein 1904, Van den Spiegel & Jangoux
1989) is not well known and must be re-
described when fresh specimens become
available.
