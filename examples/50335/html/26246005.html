ABSTRACT Surveys in Madagascar led to the discovery of three new species of dwarf Zonosaurus.

formations in the west, and the third is endemic to the low elevation humid forests in extreme south-
eastern Madagascar. The two new karst-inhabiting species are the first species of Zonosaurus known to
